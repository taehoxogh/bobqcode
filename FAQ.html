FAQs
What is the address of BOBQ in Saint Louis?
BOBQ is located at: 571 Melville Ave, University City, MO 63130, USA, University City

When is the business hour of BOBQ?
BOBQ is open from Tuesday through Sunday 12AM - 8 PM

Is the menu for BOBQ available online?
Yes, you can directly order the menu for BOBQ from this website.
Click the button “Order Online” on the top right corner to begin your order.

What are the most popular items on the BOBQ menu?
The most popular items are BOBQ COMBO and Bulgogi Beef bowl.

Does BOBQ offer delivery in Saint Louis?
No, we are currently available for pick-up orders only.

Can I add more than two proteins in one bowl?
No, we are only offering half & half options which you can add two proteins in a bowl.

Is Dine-in available at BOBQ?
Yes, absolutely. We have around 15 seats available in our place.
If you have larger number of people, please avoid most crowded times (6pm - 7pm).
